Our Electrical Services

Our expertise covers the design, supply, construction, installation, and maintenance of a wide range of electrical infrastructure, including:

Power Line Construction

Professional installation of both overhead and underground power distribution systems for reliable electricity transmission.

  • • Overhead power line installation
  • • Underground cable laying
  • • Transmission line construction
  • • Distribution network upgrades

Substation & Switchgear

Design, installation and maintenance of substations, transformers, RMUs and switchgear for efficient power distribution.

  • • Substation design and construction
  • • Transformer installation
  • • Ring Main Unit (RMU) setup
  • • Switchgear installation

Industrial Electrical Systems

Comprehensive electrical solutions for industrial plants including power distribution, automation and motor control.

  • • Plant power distribution
  • • Industrial automation systems
  • • Motor control centers
  • • Process control systems

Commercial & Domestic Installations

Safe and reliable electrical installations for commercial buildings and residential properties meeting all safety standards.

  • • Building wiring and cabling
  • • Lighting system installation
  • • Electrical safety inspections
  • • Circuit breaker panel upgrades

Solar Energy Systems

Custom solar power solutions including grid-tied and off-grid systems for residential and commercial applications.

  • • Solar panel installation
  • • Grid-tied solar systems
  • • Off-grid solar solutions
  • • Solar battery storage

Power Backup Systems

Reliable power backup solutions including stabilizers, inverters, generators and UPS systems for uninterrupted power supply.

  • • Voltage stabilizers
  • • Power inverters
  • • Generator installation
  • • Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)

Smart Metering Systems

Advanced metering infrastructure including smart meters, billing and remote monitoring systems for efficient energy management.

  • • Smart meter installation
  • • Energy monitoring systems
  • • Automated billing solutions
  • • Remote consumption tracking

Electrical Maintenance

Preventive and corrective maintenance services to ensure optimal performance and longevity of electrical systems.

  • • Routine system inspections
  • • Preventive maintenance
  • • Emergency repairs
  • • System performance optimization

HVAC Systems

Professional installation and maintenance of air conditioning systems for optimal climate control and energy efficiency.

  • • AC system installation
  • • Ventilation systems
  • • HVAC maintenance
  • • Energy efficient solutions
