About BANJ Electricals

Your trusted partner for professional electrical engineering services in Uganda and beyond

Company Background

BANJ Electricals Ltd is committed to delivering high-quality and reliable services tailored to meet the diverse needs of its clients. Our expertise spans the surveying, design, construction, and maintenance of distribution and transmission powerlines, as well as the procurement, installation, and maintenance of industrial and domestic electrical systems.

We are regulated by the Electricity Regulatory Authority (ERA) to operate on all electrical installation works under permit number ERA/EIP/CLX/024/8264. We are also prequalified by the Petroleum Authority of Uganda on the National Supplier Database (NSD) for goods, works, and services in the oil and gas sector.

At BANJ Electricals Ltd, excellence and client satisfaction are at the core of what we do.

Our Core Services

  • Power Line Construction, Surveying and Design
  • Substation Design, Construction and Maintenance
  • Supply of Electrical Materials, Tools and Equipment
  • Equipment Maintenance (UPS, Stabilizers, ACs, Generators, Inverters)
  • Cable Works (Laying, Jointing, Terminations, Testing)
  • Project Management Services
